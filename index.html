Sabel - Short Film (Division Festival of Talents 2023 - Sineliksik Winning Film)

Director: Kurt Arguelles
Co-Director: Marjorie Pulmones
Teacher Coach: Edward John Madrid, MSIT

This was the first filmmaking competition I joined to which it won first place. Festival of Talents is an annual co-curricular activity that provides an avenue to the learners to showcase their talents and skills using the learned competencies acquired at schools. It consists of different competitions such as quiz bee, painting, dancing, singing, filmmaking, etc... The event was held at Pe√±aranda Central School, Pe√±aranda, Nueva Ecija on June 3, 2023.


'Sabel' is one of my original stories I came up with, and it was inspired by the song 'Sabel' by a Filipino indie folk-pop band from Manila, 'Ben&Ben'. The whole story is about a non-binary woman who proves that being a woman doesn't mean you can't excel in men sports. The whole film was shot and edited the same day, we were given 4 hours to complete it. After winning, we moved into the Regional Festival Of Talents.

Pinta - Short Film (Regional Festival of Talents 2023 - Sineliksik 5th Place)

Director: Kurt Arguelles
Co-Director: Marjorie Pulmones
Teacher Coach: Edward John Madrid, MSIT

After winning Division Festival of Talents - Sineliksik category, we moved to the Regionals, this was held Diosdado Macapagal Government Center, San Fernando, Pampanga on June 17, 2023.


The story of 'Pinta' revolves around a father and a daughter. The father was diagnosed with dementia which makes him forget his own child, but through painting which both of them love to do, he slowly regains his memories. The message of the story that we want to show is that art is a powerful tool. This was inspired by dementia patients who recalls their memories by listening to music and by dancing.

Nikola - Short Film (TECHNO DAY 2024 1st Runner-up)

Director: Alycris Japhet Capule
Co-Director: Kurt Arguelles

This was our entry for Techno Day 2024 in Short Film category which won first runner-up. Tecno Day was an event held exclusively in the College of Information and Communications Technology at Nueva Ecija University of Science and Technology - Sumacab Campus, Cabanatuan, Nueva Ecija in the month of February. It consists of different contest that is tech-related like programming, graphic designing, and non-tech related like dancing and singing.


'Nikola' tells the story of Nikola Tesla, his contributions in the field of science.
